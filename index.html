Hedging Agricultural Risk Dashboard

Farming is a financially risky occupation. On a daily basis, farmers and ranchers are confronted with volatility in crop price, yield, and other factors that affect their fanancial returns and overall welfare. Currently, farmers and ranchers respond to these risks based on past experience, but this can be extremely chanllenging for beginning farmers and ranchers who have less than 10 years of experience.

The purpose of this dashboard is to provide farmers and ranchers with a data-driven tool to plan for the future risks ahead. The dashboard provides an estimated price by month over the next five years and a summary of the historical yields. Users can interact with the dashboard and find the information they need.
