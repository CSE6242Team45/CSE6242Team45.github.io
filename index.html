Hedging Agricultural Risk Dashboard

Farming is a financially risky occupation. On a daily basis, farmers are confronted with an ever-changing landscape of possible price, yield, and other outcomes that affect their fanancial returns and overall welfare. Currently, farmers respond to these uncerntain and random risks based on previous experience, which is costy and lagging behind. This also creates additional chanllenges for a quarter of the farmers, who have less than five years of experience.

The purpose of this Hedging Agrichultural Risk Dashboard is to provide farmers and ranchers a data-driven tool to plan for the future risks ahead. The dashboard provides an estimated price by month over the next five years and a summary of the historical yield. Farmers and ranchers can interacte with the dashboard and find the information they need.
